## What is Altura?

Altura is a next-generation asset management layer built around a simple idea:\
**bring institutional-grade trading strategies on-chain with full transparency and real-time NAV updates.**

Every vault in Altura is fully backed, fully auditable, and designed to deliver sustainable performance through:

- Delta-neutral yield strategies
- Blue chip deFi products
- Cross-venue funding-rate harvesting
- Diversified perpetuals market structures
- Automated NAV progression backed by an on-chain oracle
